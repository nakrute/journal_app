# OutLoud Content Rules

These are starter rules for the public app experience.

- Use a real profile identity.
- Share only photos and voice notes you are comfortable sending to your selected audience.
- Do not harass, threaten, impersonate, or pressure other users.
- Do not share illegal, exploitative, or non-consensual content.
- Use report and block when something feels unsafe.
- Users should be able to delete posts, remove friends, block profiles, export data, and delete their account before public launch.
